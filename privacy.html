Privacy Policy

Effective: March 15, 2025

What we do at Trendlin

Trendlin researches emerging trends from across the web — social media, forums, news outlets, industry reports, and public data — and writes clear, insightful articles about those trends. We do not create the trends; we report on them.

No accounts. No signups. No newsletters. No logins. You don't need to register anything to read our content. This Privacy Policy explains what little information we collect when you simply browse the site.

Information we collect

You do not provide any personal information to use Trendlin. There are no forms, no comment sections, no account creation, no newsletter signups, and no contact forms that store your data. You simply read.

Information automatically collected: Like most websites, when you visit Trendlin, our servers automatically receive technical information — your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring pages, and which articles you view. This helps us understand what trends our readers enjoy and improve the site.

Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies. The cookies we use include:

  • Essential cookies — Required for basic site functionality
  • Analytics cookies — Help us understand which trends our readers enjoy most (using services like Google Analytics)
  • Affiliate cookies — Track purchases made through our affiliate links (this never affects your price)

You can disable cookies through your browser settings. Since we don't have accounts or logins, disabling cookies won't affect your ability to read our content.

How we use your information

We use automatically collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve Trendlin
  • Understand which trends and topics resonate with readers
  • Track affiliate referrals and commissions (no personal identification)
  • Protect against bots and abuse

We do not send emails, newsletters, or any messages. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with anyone. We do not have user accounts, so there is nothing to "sign in" to.

Affiliate disclosure

Trendlin participates in affiliate marketing programs, including the Amazon Associates Program. This means we may earn commissions when you click on or purchase via affiliate links — at no extra cost to you. Affiliate links use cookies to track referrals, which helps keep our content free. No personal information is shared with affiliate networks beyond standard referral tracking.

Data retention

Analytics and log data are retained for a reasonable period to analyze site performance. No personal information is stored long-term. Affiliate cookie data is managed by the respective affiliate networks according to their own policies.

Third-party links

Our articles may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. Always read their privacy policies.

Children's privacy

Trendlin is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes are effective immediately upon posting. The "last updated" date at the top reflects the latest version.

This Privacy Policy applies solely to Trendlin. By using our site, you agree to this policy. Questions? Visit our site — no email needed.
